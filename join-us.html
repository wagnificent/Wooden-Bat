Rules & Regulations

The Wooden Bat Classic follows standard baseball rules with some tournament-specific regulations. All players must adhere to these rules to ensure fair play and sportsmanship.

General Tournament Rules

  • All players must be 30 years of age or older
  • Wooden bats only - no metal or composite bats allowed
  • Standard 9-inning games (time limits may apply)
  • MLB official rules apply except where modified by tournament rules
  • Sportsmanship and respect for umpires is mandatory

Player Eligibility

  • All players must provide valid identification
  • No professional players (current or within last 5 years)
  • Players may only compete for one team
  • Roster changes must be approved by tournament director

Equipment Regulations

  • Wooden bats must meet tournament specifications
  • Helmets with face guards recommended
  • Metal cleats allowed (check field regulations)
  • Uniforms must be matching for all team members
