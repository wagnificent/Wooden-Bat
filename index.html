For over 30 years, the Wooden Bat Classic has offered baseball players aged 30 and older the chance to step back onto the diamond, rekindle their love for the game, and compete in a premier wooden bat tournament. Set against the backdrop of sunny Fort Myers, Florida, this annual event brings together teams and free agents from across the country for a week of camaraderie, competition, and the pure joy of baseball.
